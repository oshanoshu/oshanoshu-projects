Introduction

Lionel Messi is an Argentine who plays for FC Barcelona as a forward since getting into the club at the age of 13. He was a child prodigy, and everyone started talking about him early in his career. He lived beyond the expectations of the club and has been acknowledged by many as the best ever to grace this game of football.

Goals For Club and Country

For Club

  • Laliga: 387 Goals in 421 Appearances
  • Champions League: 100 Goals in 125 Appearances
  • Other Competitions: 69 Goals in 95 Appearances

For Country

  • 65 Goals in 128 Appearances

Major Honors

  • FIFA Player of the Year: 5 times (4 times in a row)
  • La Liga Best Player of the Year: 7 times (5 times in a row)
  • Argentine Footballer of the Year: 11 times (7 times in a row)
  • FIFA FIFPro World XI: 11 times in a row
  • FIFA World Cup Golden Ball: 1 (2014)
